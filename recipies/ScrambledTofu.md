# Scrambled Tofu
- 14oz package extra firm tofu drained
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tbsp hing
- 1/2 cup nutritional yeast
- salt/ pepper
- veggies

- Mash the tofu and saute in the oil
- After a few minutes, add the remaining ingredients and toss until well cooked
